Controls:

up/down = drive
left/right = turn
shift + left/right = turn slowly
use + up/down in air = flip

- hurts from fall over 7 blocks
- explodes when it dies
- must be contain petrol (fuel) before you can drive it
you dies if you fails in a flip

Cars:

- Give it a block to change its texture
- Give it glass to change its glass
You gets back the block when you changing to another texture
Note that all blocks doesn't work, like animated blocks because those usually have another kind of texture
